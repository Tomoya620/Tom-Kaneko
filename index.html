Experience of Taiwanese wedding

I haven't attended Taiwanese wedding before, so this was my first time to go through this kind of experience. My parents and my cousins also attended the wedding with me. During the wedding, I found out that lots of things were different compared to the wedding I had participated before.

When people show up in a wedding, they are usually blessing for the bride and groom. For me, there are many reasons why I attending this wedding. One of the reasons is that I want to see that what is the difference of wedding between each culture. Another reason is that this wedding is for my parents' friends, so due to this relationship, I need to attend it.

Before the bride and the groom entered the banquet, the host played a short video about the childhood of the bride and groom. Soon after the video, they started to serve us the dishes. They were all tasty and extraordinary for me.

Here are some of the food that I have enjoyed among all.

Even though the cuisines were wonderful, after they served all the dishes, I started to take out my cellphone for taking some pictures and then started using it.

Throughout the wedding, I chatted with many people, even with the people that I have never met before. The host created a wonderful atmosphere and everyone enjoyed it.

This is my first time for attending this kind of wedding, although it was quite different than what I thought, I still find it interesting. If there is another opportunity for me to attend the Taiwanese wedding, I would definitely join it and get more understanding of Taiwanese culture.
